00248 //====== additional information ======
00249 /*
00250 
00251 -       when a node child is a solid leaf the node child number is zero
00252 -       two adjacent areas (sharing a plane at opposite sides) share a face
00253         this face is a portal between the areas
00254 -       when an area uses a face from the faceindex with a positive index
00255         then the face plane normal points into the area
00256 -       the face edges are stored counter clockwise using the edgeindex
00257 -       two adjacent convex areas (sharing a face) only share One face
00258         this is a simple result of the areas being convex
00259 -       the areas can't have a mixture of ground and gap faces
00260         other mixtures of faces in one area are allowed
00261 -       areas with the AREACONTENTS_CLUSTERPORTAL in the settings have
00262         the cluster number set to the negative portal number
00263 -       edge zero is a dummy
00264 -       face zero is a dummy
00265 -       area zero is a dummy
00266 -       node zero is a dummy
00267 */
